Welcome to Dinosaur Museum Catalog

I have organized the countries into rectangular boxes where the width of each box indicates the number of dinosaurs present in that country.

Each dinosaur is represented by a line, with the length of the line indicating the dinosaur's height in meters.

At the end of the line, an image is displayed indicating whether the dinosaur was Herbivorous, Carnivorous, or Omnivorous.

You can click on a line (dinosaur) to know more about it.

Marks: Line Channels: Length of the line, Area of rectangle, Color hue, Shape Interactions: Hover, Click
